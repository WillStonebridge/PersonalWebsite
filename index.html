He keeps a Chicago Cubs W Flag near his desk to remind him, sometimes it only takes 108 years of failure (and maybe Kris Bryant) to succeed.

Despite his professor's best efforts, Will occasionally finds time to pursue his hobbies. Whether it's exploring Classical History, learning guitar and piano or brushing up on his Japanese with Duolingo, Will finds passion in teaching himself new things.

Will Stonebridge has done quite a bit. Hopefully, he can work for you next.
